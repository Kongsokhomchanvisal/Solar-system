The Solar System

OUTER PLANETS

The outer planets are gas giants Jupiter and Saturn, and ice giants Uranus and Neptune. Beyond Neptune, a newer class of smaller world called dwarf planets reign, including longtime favorite Pluto

PLANET THUMBNAIL DESCRIPTION
Jupiter the fifth planet from the sun. Jupiter is the biggest planet in the Solar System. Jupiter is made of gases. that Big Red Spot on Jupiter is a giant storm.
Saturn The sixth planet from the sun. Saturn has big rings around it. The Rings are made of rock and ice. Three of Saturn's rings can be seen from Earth.
Uranus The seventh planet from the Sun. Uranus is the only planet to spin around on its side. it would take you eight and a half years to reach Uranus in a space rocket.
Neptune The eighth planet from the sun. neptune has four ring and 11 moons around it. Space rocket can't find landed on Neptune because Neptune is made of gas.
